### Slide 1 — Title
**HUMAN DATA RIGHTS**
Editorial & Media Strategy
*Phase 1 | July–August 2026*

**Objective:** Establish Human Data Rights as a recognizable term in public AI discourse through consistent, high-quality editorial media.

---

### Slide 2 — Our Mission
Human Data Rights exists to put humanity at the center of the AI conversation.

We are **not anti-AI.** We believe AI will become one of humanity's most transformative technologies.

Our role is to ensure that as AI evolves, people understand their **rights · role · contribution · agency.**

> We don't tell people what to think. We help them ask better questions.

---

### Slide 3 — Success in Year One
**Primary KPI:** increase public awareness of the term *Human Data Rights.*

Success means:
- HDR becomes a recognizable phrase.
- Journalists and creators reference HDR.
- More people discover and discuss the concept.
- Strong foundations for future coalitions.

**Secondary goals:** partnerships · coalitions · newsletter growth · grants & sustainable funding · trusted editorial voice.

---

### Slide 4 — Editorial Philosophy
Everything begins with one question:
> **What is this week's Human Question?**

- Every opinion should be defendable.
- Every argument should originate from research.
- The newsletter is the editorial source of truth.
- Everything else distributes that thinking.
